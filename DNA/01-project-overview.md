# GnoccaTrack — Project Overview

## What It Is

GnoccaTrack is a mobile-first Progressive Web App (PWA) designed for nightlife discovery. Users tap a single button — "Qui Gnocca" — to signal that a bar, pub, or venue is buzzing with energy right now. That signal accumulates with other users' signals into a real-time hotspot visible on an interactive map.

The app is anonymous, fast, fun, and aimed at the 16–30 age group in Italy.

## Core Concept

"Gnocca" = nightlife energy, social atmosphere, movement, venue activity.

The app is **not** a system for identifying, tracking, rating, photographing, or targeting real people. It is a collective crowd-signal about places.

## What Users Can Do

1. **See the map** — fullscreen map centered on their GPS location, showing pulsing hotspots at nearby venues
2. **Signal atmosphere** — tap "Qui Gnocca" to add their energy to a venue's hotspot
3. **See live intensity** — hotspots pulse with different animations based on current activity (low / medium / high / bomb)
4. **Stay anonymous** — nickname + 4-digit PIN only. No email, no phone, no real identity required
5. **Check their profile** — see their badge, click count, and recent Gnocca actions
6. **Switch themes** — dark mode (default) and light mode

## Key Design Principles

- Map IS the app. The map takes 100% of the viewport.
- One-tap main action. The flow from app open → "Qui Gnocca" → confirmed click is 3 taps max.
- Anonymous by design. No personal data ever required.
- Mobile-first. Designed for 390px screens. Touch targets ≥ 44px.
- Free to operate. No paid APIs in the baseline stack.
